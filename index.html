First Walkthrough

  1. Start blank. Click or drag cells to draw a seed.
  2. Paused only: click a lit cell once to turn it off.
  3. Click a pattern to turn Stamp on, then place as many copies as you want.
  4. Press Play and watch what survives, travels, or explodes.

Cells
